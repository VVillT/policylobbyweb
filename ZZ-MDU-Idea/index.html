How it works

Join the movement in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us which bills concerns you

You are not alone! MDU will pool together medical professional with similar concerns. The system will then list out the key people in government/ MPs to influence and how many needs to be contacted!

02

Make your contribution

Once you have contacted a MP/ government body, you can then submit your record anonymously to our enterprise graded secure system. We will centralise the anonymised information and gauge if all relevant MPs and bodies are contacted to maxmise the chance of lobbying

03

Revise your view and actions

You will be given access to the collective lobbying reporting in the form of weekly news feed and dashboard. We endeavour to give you the latest update so that you can constantly update your views and make decision on dialing up your lobbying effort or not - with our supports!
